He is 36 years old.He was born on june 24, 1987 in Argentina Rosario.Messi grew up in a poor, working-class family, where his father was a factory steelworker and his mother a cleaner (Sukumar). The future for the Messi family did not look bright, but Lionel's talent in soccer at a young age was noticed by his parents, and they thought soccer would provide a way out of poverty.
